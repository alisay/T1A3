About

Alisa Blakeney curates exhibitions, codes software, writes texts, and joins clubs to take part in and understand the changes computing makes to culture. She co-directed the Perth artist-run space Paper Mountain (2013-2015), worked as a curator at Bunbury Regional Art Gallery (2014-2018) and was a co-founder of Compiler: a London-based platform for the production and presentation of new media art and dialogue (2017).
